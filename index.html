Strategies to Get Out of Debt

Use the 20% calculated above applying one of these proven methodologies.

Snowball Method

List your debts from smallest to largest balance (ignoring interest rates). Pay minimums on all, and attack the smallest debt with everything left.

Avalanche Method

Mathematically most efficient. List debts by highest interest rate. Focus all extra cash on the debt with the highest rate to save on interest.

Consolidation

Swap expensive debts (like credit cards) for a personal loan with a lower interest rate. Call your creditors and offer a lump-sum cash settlement.
